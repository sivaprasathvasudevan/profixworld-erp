🔄 Replacement & Returns

Every item carries a 7-day replacement for manufacturing defects. If a product stops working correctly within 7 days of delivery due to a fault (not physical/liquid damage or misuse), we replace it free — bring it or message us on WhatsApp with your order number.

  • Applies to genuine manufacturing defects only.
  • Item must be in its original condition with the box/accessories.
  • Physical damage, water damage, and misuse aren't covered.
